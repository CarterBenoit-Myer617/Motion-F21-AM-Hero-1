2022 PHS Philadelphia Flower Show Location, Date, and Theme are Announced!

Welcome to the 2022 Flower Show "In Full Bloom"

Chosen to showcase how the beauty in nature restores all of us, “In Full Bloom” welcomes all on a journey to explore the restorative and healing power of nature and plants. This year’s theme promotes good health, positive well-being, and a passion for life that culminates in a gorgeous and colorful spectacle. The PHS Philadelphia Flower Show will return to FDR Park in 2022 Saturday, June 11 – Sunday, June 19
